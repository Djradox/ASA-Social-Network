A local, agile and human-sized organization

In fact, we are physically present in Calais. This allow us to be at the heart of cross-Channel exchanges. This unavoidable location engage us to propose a complete expertise. In addition, the responsiveness of our 30 employees makes them available to you 7 days a week, 24 hours a day.

Your multi-skills Brexit correspondent

ASA performs on Mandate the creation of import / export declarations as well as the handling of transits. The customs clerks present at the Port of Calais and at the Tunnel follow the sanitary inspections and veterinary checks carried out by SIVEP. We are therefore present at the various stages of import and export, which offers you upstream and downstream expertise in all stages of pre-routing and post-routing of transport for Brexit.
